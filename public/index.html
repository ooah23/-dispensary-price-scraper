An eighth is 3.5 grams — one-eighth of an ounce. It's the most popular purchase size in NYC dispensaries and a good starting point for most buyers. Expect 6–10 small bowls or joints from a typical eighth. A quarter (7g) and half-ounce (14g) offer better per-gram value but require a bigger upfront spend.
Flower is the dried cannabis bud you smoke in a bowl, joint, or blunt. It's the most natural form and the cheapest per session at licensed dispensaries. Vape carts (cartridges) are pre-filled oil cartridges used with a 510-thread battery. They're discrete, odor-reduced, and easy to dose — but typically more expensive per gram than flower. If you're new, starting with a small pre-roll or a 0.5g cart is a low-commitment way to try both.
Start small. A single pre-roll (0.5–1g joint) costs $8–15 and lets you try a strain before committing to a larger quantity. If you prefer vaping, a 0.5g cart is similarly low-risk. Avoid buying a full ounce on your first visit — you may not enjoy that strain after the novelty wears off.
