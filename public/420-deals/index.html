Which NYC dispensaries have 4/20 deals in 2026?

Most of the 20 NYC licensed dispensaries we track run 4/20 promotions on whole-flower eighths and ounces. Housing Works Cannabis, Alchemy NYC, Green Genius, New Amsterdam, and Dazed have all run 4/20 events in prior years. Check the price table above for today's lowest prices — we update daily.

How much does an eighth cost in NYC on 4/20?

Eighth-ounce (3.5g) prices at NYC licensed dispensaries range from $28 to $60 on a normal day. On 4/20, promotions can push prices lower — our tracker shows the real current price so you know whether a "deal" is actually below the baseline.

What is the cheapest dispensary in NYC?

Are 4/20 dispensary deals real or just marketing?

It depends on the store. Some dispensaries offer genuine 15–30% discounts on 4/20; others dress up normal prices as "deals." Our price tracker compares today's prices against historical data, so you can see actual price drops versus baseline.

Do NYC dispensaries require ID on 4/20?

Yes — all licensed NYC dispensaries require a valid government-issued ID proving you are 21+. This applies every day, including 4/20. Unlicensed smoke shops do not have this requirement but are not legal retailers.

Where are the 4/20 events in NYC 2026?

Washington Square Park hosts the largest 4/20 gathering in NYC. Union Square and Prospect Park in Brooklyn also see significant gatherings. Many dispensaries host in-store events with raffles, live music, and product launches. Check individual dispensary Instagram accounts closer to April 20.
