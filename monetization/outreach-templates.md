Most dispensaries I've spoken to see a measurable uptick in foot traffic within the first two weeks — people are already comparison-shopping online, this just makes sure they choose you.

I'd love to hop on a 10-minute call to show you the dashboard and answer any questions. Does sometime this week work?

Best,
[Your Name]

## Phone Call Script (30-second pitch)

"Hi, is this the manager or owner? Great — my name is [Name], I run NYCWeedPrice.org, a price comparison site for NYC dispensaries. Your store is already listed and showing up when people compare prices before they buy.

I'm calling because I'm offering a small number of stores a Featured placement — basically, you show up first when someone searches for flower near you. It's $299 a month, and I send you a weekly report showing exactly how many people clicked through to your store.

Do you have two minutes to hear more, or is there a better time to call back?"

### Notes for Outreach
- Lead with Featured ($299) — easiest yes, lowest friction
- Partner ($699) is the upsell conversation after 30 days of proof
- Premium ($999) is for multi-location operators (Gotham, The Alchemy)
- Always offer a **2-week free trial** of Featured to get them on the hook
- Early-adopter framing: "I'm only doing this rate for the first 5 stores"
